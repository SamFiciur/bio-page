sam ficiur


Sam Ficiur is a first year Multimedia Production student at Lethbridge College. They are curious to learn how to make video games. Their want to learn more about what goes into developing a game is what drove them to attend Lethbridge College.


They have been studying art since 2011 and started to learn how to code in 2020. Sam has completed two short visual novels and published them online. Since then they have been working on improving their art skills.


Sam was born in Lethbridge, Alberta in 1999 before moving to Coaldale in 2003. There they attended school, participating in school activities like theatre and music. Sam studied singing from 2009 to 2017, in school and in private lessons. They performed in various concerts and plays, including participating in the Kiwanis Music Festival in Lethbridge in 2013 and 2014.


After graduating from Kate Andrews High School, they took time off to do freelance work for private clients while improving their art skills. They enjoy making art and playing music, and hope to make their own game someday.
